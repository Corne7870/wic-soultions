Intelligent Entry Management

WIC Solutions provides state-of-the-art access control solutions that allow you to manage and monitor movement throughout your building or estate. From simple card readers to advanced biometric systems and smartphone integration, we design systems that offer both security and convenience.

Our solutions allow for granular control over who can enter specific areas at different times. We integrate these systems with your existing infrastructure, providing a centralized platform for reporting and administration. Whether you need to secure a single office or a multi-site facility, our team delivers a scalable solution.

Our Solutions Include

  • Biometric fingerprint and facial recognition
  • Proximity card and key fob systems
  • Smartphone-based mobile credentials
  • Intercom and visitor management systems
  • Cloud-based access management
  • Time and attendance tracking integration
