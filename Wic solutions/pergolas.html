Beautiful Outdoor Pergola Structures

WIC Solutions designs and builds pergolas that transform outdoor spaces into comfortable, shaded entertainment areas. Whether you need a freestanding structure or one attached to your home, our team creates pergolas that complement your property's architecture and withstand the South African elements.

We work with premium composite materials, aluminium, and quality timber to build structures that are both beautiful and low-maintenance. Every pergola is custom-designed to fit your space and lifestyle needs.

Pergola Options

  • Freestanding and attached pergolas
  • Composite and aluminium structures
  • Timber pergolas with modern finishes
  • Adjustable louvre systems
  • Integrated lighting and ceiling fans
  • Custom sizes and configurations
