Stay Warm from the Ground Up

Underfloor heating is the most efficient and comfortable way to heat your home or commercial space. WIC Solutions installs both electric and hydronic underfloor heating systems that distribute warmth evenly, eliminate cold spots, and reduce energy consumption compared to traditional heating methods.

Our team handles everything from system design and floor preparation to installation and thermostat programming. We work with all floor types including tile, stone, laminate, and engineered wood.

Systems We Install

  • Electric mat underfloor heating
  • Electric cable systems
  • Hydronic (water-based) systems
  • Smart thermostat integration
  • Compatible with tile, stone, laminate, and wood floors
  • New build and retrofit installations
