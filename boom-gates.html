Automated Vehicle Access Control

WIC Solutions provides professional installation and maintenance of automated boom gates for residential estates, commercial properties, and industrial facilities. Our systems are designed for high-frequency use, ensuring reliable vehicle access control while maintaining property security.

We offer a range of boom arm lengths and speeds to suit different entrance requirements. Our installations include all necessary safety features such as induction loops, safety beams, and can be integrated with various access control methods including RFID, biometric systems, and license plate recognition.

Features & Solutions

  • Heavy-duty automated boom barriers
  • Rapid-action booms for high-traffic areas
  • Manual override for power failures
  • Integration with RFID and biometric readers
  • Induction loop and safety beam installation
  • Regular maintenance and repair services
