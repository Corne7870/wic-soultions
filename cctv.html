HD Surveillance & Remote Monitoring

WIC Solutions designs and installs high-definition CCTV systems that provide comprehensive visual coverage of your property. Our IP-based camera solutions offer crystal-clear imagery, even in low-light conditions, and can be accessed remotely from your smartphone or tablet anywhere in the world.

We provide a range of camera options including dome, bullet, and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras to suit different environments. Our installations include network video recorders (NVRs) with high-capacity storage for extended video retention and AI-powered motion detection to reduce false alerts.

Our CCTV Solutions

  • HD and 4K IP camera installations
  • Night vision and thermal imaging cameras
  • Remote viewing via mobile app and desktop
  • AI-powered motion and perimeter detection
  • Integrated network storage (NVR) solutions
  • Maintenance and cloud-storage backup-options
