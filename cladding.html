Modern Cladding & Screen Solutions

Enhance the exterior and interior of your property with our range of cladding and screening products. WIC Solutions installs composite slat screens, aluminium cladding panels, and timber feature walls that add both privacy and architectural interest to any space.

Our screening solutions are perfect for creating private outdoor areas, concealing service equipment, or adding a contemporary design element to facades. We work with durable, low-maintenance materials suited to the South African climate.

Products & Applications

  • Composite slat screening
  • Aluminium cladding panels
  • Timber feature walls
  • Privacy screens for outdoor areas
  • Facade cladding systems
  • Balustrade and pool fencing infills
