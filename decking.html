Outdoor Decking Solutions

WIC Solutions supplies and installs the most trusted brands of composite and timber decking. Whether you're creating a pool deck, patio extension, or rooftop terrace, our team delivers beautiful, durable outdoor living spaces that are built to last in the South African climate.

We work with leading composite brands including Trex, Eva-Last, Fiberon, and MoistureShield, as well as quality hardwood timber options. Every installation is precision-fitted to your space with proper substructure engineering.

Our Decking Range

  • Composite decking (Trex, Eva-Last, Fiberon, MoistureShield)
  • Timber and hardwood decking
  • Pool surrounds and elevated decks
  • Balcony and rooftop decking
  • Substructure design and engineering
  • Maintenance and restoration services
