Entry Point Screening Solutions

WIC Solutions provides high-sensitivity walk-through and hand-held metal detection systems designed for efficiency and accuracy. Our systems are ideal for high-traffic environments where security cannot be compromised, such as airports, hotels, and public venues.

We install multi-zone walk-through detectors that precisely locate metallic objects on a person, reducing false alarms and improving throughput. Our solutions include full setup, calibration, and training for your security personnel.

  • High-sensitivity hand-held scanners
  • Weatherproof outdoor models
  • Portable and rapid-deployment units
  • Automatic counting and reporting features
  • System calibration and certification
