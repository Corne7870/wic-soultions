Professional Screening Solutions

WIC Solutions specializes in the supply, installation, and technical support of high-resolution X-ray screening machines. Designed for use in hotels, commercial properties, and high-security government buildings, our systems provide clear, detailed images to identify prohibited items in luggage and parcels.

Our team ensures that your screening equipment is installed correctly and integrated into your security protocol. we provide full operator training and ongoing technical maintenance to ensure your facility remains secure and compliant with safety standards.

Key Features

  • Dual-energy imaging for material discrimination
  • High-resolution parcel and baggage screening
  • Advanced threat detection software
  • Real-time image manipulation tools
  • Operator training and certification
  • Preventative maintenance and 24/7 support
